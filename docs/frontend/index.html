IIUI Campus Recovery Guide

Get your lost belongings back in 4 simple, secure steps:

1

1. Sign Up & Verify

Create an account with your email. We will instantly send a real 6-digit verification code (OTP) to your inbox to activate your profile.

2

2. Post Lost / Found

Report what you lost or found. While you type, our smart scanner searches the database in real-time to alert you of potential matches!

3

3. Submit Claim Proof

Found your item in the listings? Tap "Claim", write a brief proof description, and upload your Student Card to show it belongs to you.

4

4. Chat & Meet Up

Once approved, a private chat room opens! Chat securely with the finder to arrange a safe meetup on campus and get your item back.

Quick Guide: What does each section do?

My Reported Items See the items you have posted. You can edit details, check smart matching suggestions, or delete posts once items are returned.
Claims Received Review requests from students claiming ownership of items you found. Check their proof description and Student Card, then approve or reject them.
Claims Submitted Keep track of all claims you filed for lost items found by others. You can monitor if the finder has approved or rejected your requests.
Messages (Chats) Once a claim is approved, a private chat room opens! Message the other student safely here to coordinate when and where to meet up on campus.
